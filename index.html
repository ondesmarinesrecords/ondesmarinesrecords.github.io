Ondesmarines Records began as the personal label of the artist Yuri Afasia.

Over time, it has grown into a platform for collaborative works and compilation releases, creating a space where diverse creative voices can meet and resonate.

The label exists not to control or define creativity, but to protect and respect artists’ freedom. It serves as a framework that allows creation to remain open, fluid, and sincere.

Active since 2015, Ondesmarines Records engages in artistic projects and showcase performances across multiple formats and mediums.

It is not confined by genre or form.
It moves where creativity flows.
